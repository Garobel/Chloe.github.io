🌑 Basic Moon Facts 🌑

1. Earth's Trusted Friend 🌍

The moon is tided to Earth for billions of years. It follows us as we journey through space.

2. Size Matters! 📏

The moon may look small in the night sky but it's actually quite big. In fact, it's about one-fourth the size of Earth. Imagine a giant cosmic beach ball floating above us!

3. Moon's Distance Dance 🕺💃

The moon isn't always the same distance from Earth. It orbits us in an elliptical shape. It's about 238,855 miles away. That's a lot of distance for a dance partner!

4. Lunar Landscapes 🌄

The moon's surface is covered in fascinating features, including craters, mountains, and plains.

5. One Side Show 🎭

we always see the same face of the moon from Earth. This is because the moon rotates on its axis at the same rate it orbits our planet. So, one side is always hidden from our view.

6. Moonlight Magic ✨

The moon doesn't produce its own light; it reflects the sunlight that reaches it. This reflection is what we see as moonlight.
